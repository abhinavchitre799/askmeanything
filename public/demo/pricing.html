Pricing questions

The short answers. More in our FAQ.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes. Pro includes a 14-day free trial with every feature unlocked, and no credit card is required to start.

How does per-user billing work?

You're billed only for active members. Guests are free and don't count toward your seat total.

Can I change plans later?

Anytime. Upgrades apply immediately; downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit cards and, on annual Business plans, payment by invoice.
