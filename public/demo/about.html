About us

We build calm software

Driftboard helps small teams do focused work without drowning in features.

Driftboard was founded in 2021 by two former agency leads who were tired of project tools that did everything except help you finish the work. We started with a simple idea: a board, a few tasks, and clear owners — and the discipline to stop there.

Today we're a fully remote team of just over 40 people, headquartered in Austin, Texas, serving more than 12,000 teams around the world. We're profitable, independent, and intend to stay that way so we can keep building for our customers instead of chasing the next funding round.

Security & trust

Security isn't an afterthought. All customer data is encrypted in transit and at rest, hosted on AWS in the United States, and backed up daily. Driftboard is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Business customers can enable SSO/SAML, audit logs, and advanced permissions. You can request our security whitepaper and latest SOC 2 report from the team.
