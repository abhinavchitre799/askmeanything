Features

Built for focus, not busywork

Driftboard gives small teams just enough structure to stay aligned — and gets out of the way.

🗂️

Boards & tasks

Organize work into boards with To-do, Doing, and Done columns. Each task supports owners, due dates, checklists, attachments up to 25 MB, labels, and threaded comments.

📅

Timeline view

Flip any board into a Gantt-style timeline to plan deadlines and spot dependencies. Timeline view is available on the Pro and Business plans.

🔁

Recurring tasks & automations

Set tasks to repeat daily, weekly, or monthly, and use simple rules like "when moved to Done, notify the owner" — no scripting required.

🔔

Reminders & daily digest

Automatic reminders fire before a task is due. Each morning, everyone gets a digest of what's assigned to them and what's overdue.

🔌

Integrations

Connect Slack, GitHub, Google Calendar, Figma, and Zapier. GitHub commits can move linked tasks automatically. Integrations are included on Pro and Business.

📱

Mobile & offline

Native iOS and Android apps stay in sync across devices and keep working offline — changes sync automatically when you reconnect.

👥

Permissions & roles

Invite teammates as Admins, Members, or Guests. Business adds advanced permissions and board-level access controls.

🔒

Security & compliance

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). Driftboard is SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and takes encrypted backups every day. Business plans add SSO/SAML and audit logs.
