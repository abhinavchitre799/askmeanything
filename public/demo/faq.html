Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about using Driftboard. Can't find it? Ask the chat assistant in the corner.

What is Driftboard?

Driftboard is a lightweight project tracker for small teams. You organize work into visual boards with tasks, owners, and due dates, and can switch to a timeline view to plan deadlines.

Is there a free plan?

Yes. The Free plan supports up to 3 members, 2 boards, and 100 tasks, and never expires. Paid plans start at $9 per user per month.

How long is the free trial?

Pro comes with a 14-day free trial. Every feature is unlocked during the trial and no credit card is required to start. If you don't upgrade, your workspace drops back to the Free plan.

Which integrations are supported?

Driftboard integrates with Slack, GitHub, Google Calendar, Figma, and Zapier. Integrations are available on the Pro and Business plans.

Do you have mobile apps?

Yes — native apps for iOS and Android. They work offline and sync automatically when you reconnect.

How is my data secured?

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). Driftboard is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant, with encrypted backups taken daily. Business plans add SSO/SAML and audit logs.

Where is my data stored?

Customer data is hosted on Amazon Web Services in the United States (us-east-1), with daily encrypted backups.

Can I import from another tool?

Yes. You can import boards from Trello and Asana, or from a CSV file, during setup. Our team can help with larger migrations.

How do I cancel, and can I get a refund?

You can cancel anytime from Settings → Billing with no cancellation fee. All paid plans include a 30-day money-back guarantee — email support within 30 days for a full refund.

What are your support hours?

Email support is available on every plan and we reply within one business day. Support hours are Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm US Eastern. Business plans get priority support and live chat.

How do I contact support?

Email support@driftboard.example or use the in-app chat. For sales and Business plans, contact sales@driftboard.example.
